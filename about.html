Our Goal:

Our goal isn't just teaching web dev - it's making a tangible impact on the world. In a world increasingly full of technology, it's more important than ever to have a tech presence and know the basics of technology. That's why MSJ Web Dev is here! We teach people about web dev so they're prepared for the future of technology. We'll be teaching the skills necessary to develop your own website from scratch so you can benefit your own community. We also want to support the community in our own way - we plan to build a website for non-profits and small businesses every few months.
